# PRD: PharmaPace PR - True Cost of Living Analyzer

## Vision

To provide Puerto Rico's pharmaceutical professionals with an expert-level tool that deconstructs job offers beyond the base salary. This tool empowers candidates to negotiate effectively by surfacing the hidden economic realities of the island (energy costs, commute, and company performance).

## Core Requirements

- **TCOL Analysis**: Real-time integration of Puerto Rico's specific cost factors (LUMA utility rates, AutoExpreso tolls, fuel prices).
- **Recruiter Intelligence**: Evaluate offers against specific company tiers (Tier 1 like Amgen/AbbVie vs. others).
- **Compliance Alignment**: Weight roles based on high-demand skills like GAMP5, FDA 21 CFR Part 11, and CSA/CSV.
- **Relocation Feasibility**: Calculate ROI on moving based on municipality-specific housing and traffic models.

## User Experience (UX)

- Professional, high-trust interface using "Pharma-Blue" and "Clean-White" aesthetics.
- Quick "One-Click" CV analysis.
- Clear, actionable negotiation strategies.
