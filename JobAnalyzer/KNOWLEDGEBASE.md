# PharmaPace PR: Knowledge Base (KB)

## 🏛️ Industry Standards & Regulatory Framework

This tool operates within the specific regulatory context of the Puerto Rico Life Sciences hub.

### 1. ALCOA+ Data Integrity

All reports generated by PharmaPace PR follow the FDA's ALCOA+ principles:

- **Attributable**: Reports are session-attributed and include a Unique System ID.
- **Legible**: Structured JSON output ensures high readability in PDF format.
- **Contemporaneous**: Every analysis is stamped with the exact date/time (Atlantic Standard Time).
- **Original**: Analysis is unique to the specific CV and Job Opportunity provided.
- **Accurate**: Logic is grounded in current PR industrial market data.

## 🏎️ Commute & Logistics Benchmarks

- **Gasoline**: Puerto Rico uses metric liters. Current March 2026 average for Regular Gas is **$0.92/L**.
- **Distance**: The tool converts all KM estimates to **Miles** (1km = 0.621mi) for standard US/PR business reporting.
- **Tolls**: Primary routes accounted for include PR-22 (AutoExpreso) and PR-52 (Dynamic pricing).

## ⚡ Energy & Infrastructure (The LUMA Factor)

- **Residential Electricity**: PR has one of the highest rates in the US.
- **Benchmark**: Current rate is **$0.26 per kWh** (Temporary reduction approved by PREB for Q1 2026; previous benchmark was $0.33/kWh).
- **Impact**: Candidates relocating should be aware of rate volatility when evaluating "Gross" salary packages.

## 💼 PR Contract Types (Legal/Internal)

### W2 (Full-Time Employee)

- **Taxes**: FICA (7.65% employer matched).
- **Benefits**: Christmas Bonus (Bono de Navidad), Vacation/Sick leave mandated by Act 4-2017.

### 1099 (Independent Contractor)

- **Taxes**: Candidate pays the full Self-Employment tax (15.3%).
- **Equivalent**: Generally requires 25-30% higher gross pay to match W2 net.

### Form 480 (Professional Services)

- **Tax Withholding**: Standard 10% withholding unless an exemption certificate is provided.
- **Act 60**: Potential for 4% flat tax rate for professional service providers exporting services or specific specialized roles.
